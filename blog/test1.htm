Meet Aquarium

Hello world! This is my first blog post on my prototype content manager, Aquarium! I built this for two reasons: 1) to show my skills as a developer/designer and 2) because I wanted a good middle ground between Wordpress and Carrd. I couldn't find one, so I just made my own.

Visually it's servicable and very much designed for mobile devices, but under the hood, I can add content files, blog files and assets while the site auto-generates hyperlinks and formatting accordingly, all without needing a heavy frontend. Ideal for content creators with low bandwidth or limited workstation access (like me). The codebase is small as well to save costs on webspace and deployment.

As for the name, I rechristened the site "The Fish Tank" after a visual pun I drew.
